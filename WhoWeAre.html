...What's behind

We are two Italian friends: one hails from the picturesque coastal region of Liguria, known for its fragrant olive groves and artisanal olive oils.
The other originates from the charming countryside of Abruzzo, celebrated for its rich vineyards and exquisite wines.
United by a deep-rooted passion for exceptional culinary experiences, we have embarked on a journey to bring the finest extra virgin olive oils and grapessed oils from Italy to Holland. Our mission is to share the authentic flavors and traditions of our homeland, ensuring that every bottle we offer embodies the essence of quality and craftsmanship. Through dedication to sourcing from trusted producers and a commitment to excellence, we invite you to join us in savoring the essence of Italy, one bottle at a time
